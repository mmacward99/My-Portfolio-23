PHILADELPHIA EAGLES 2022

Black Helmet Release


For this campaign, our team worked on developing a landing page and a mobile app experience for the Philadelphia Eagles black helmet release. I primarily worked on the landing page, adding assets such as our helmet release video, exclusive black helmet photoshoot, and more. I created the landing page header using Adobe Photoshop.




Problem Statement

The black helmet was the Eagles first alternate since 2010. Creating an informative webpage that allowed fans to see the new helmets in every facet was very important.

Goal

Our goal with this webpage and app experience was to get fans involved in the excitement of these new alternate helmets. We wanted to follow the theme of our Black Helmet Release by created a webpage and app experience that followed suite.


Design Process

The design process of this campaign was fairly simple. My supervisor and I worked together to create a simple and informative web page and app experience. We used Figma to prototype, Judo to create the app experience, and our Content Managemnt System, Pocket to create the webpage.

Conclusion

My team was very please with the way the app experience and web page turned out. We stayed consisant with a black theme and created an experience that was a one page scroll to ensure that fans would not miss a moment of the thrilling new alternate!
