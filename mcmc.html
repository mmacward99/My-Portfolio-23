My Cause My Cleats

Every year, NFL players reveal their passions beyond the game and wear their hearts on their feet through My Cause My Cleats. Players have the opportunity to pick a cause that is important to them and represent their chosen organization on custom designed cleats. I had the opportunity to be a part of creating a landing page and app experience for the My Cause My Cleats initiative.
