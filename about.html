Melissa Ward


Hi, my name is Melissa Ward. I'm a UX/UI and Graphic Designer constantly looking to grow my knowledge and capabilities. I have a passion for creativity and a strong desire to use my skills to improve the lives of others for the better. I am adaptable, hardworking, and optimistic. In the past seven months I have served as Web Seasonal Associate for NFC Champions, the Philadelphia Eagles. Working with the outstanding group of creatives at Eagles Entertainment has been a rewarding experience, to say the least.


I grew up right outside of Philadelphia, where I attended a local high school with an outstanding art program. I started the program freshman year and continued into my senior year. I found a love for all things creative and contined to pursue that passion at James Madison Univeristy where I studied Media, Arts, and Design, concentrating in Interactive Design.
